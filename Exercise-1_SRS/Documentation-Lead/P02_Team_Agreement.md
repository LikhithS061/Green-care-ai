## Team Agreement

### Meeting Norms

- Weekly sync meetings on **Fridays at 4 PM**
- All team members must attend unless prior notice given
- Meetings will be conducted via Google Meet or in-person

### Decision Making

- **Majority vote** for non-critical decisions
- **Mentor has final say** on technical disputes
- All major decisions documented in meeting notes

### Communication Channels

| Channel        | Purpose                              | Response Time   |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------ | --------------- |
| WhatsApp Group | Urgent updates, quick questions      | Within 2 hours  |
| GitHub Issues  | Task tracking, bug reports           | Within 24 hours |
| Email          | Formal communication, mentor updates | Within 48 hours |

### Version Control Practices

1. **Feature branch workflow** - no direct commits to main
2. Branch naming: `feature/[feature-name]` or `fix/[bug-name]`
3. Pull requests require at least 1 reviewer approval
4. Commit messages follow conventional format

### Conflict Resolution

1. Direct discussion between involved parties
2. If unresolved, escalate to Project Manager
3. Final escalation to Project Mentor

### Work Distribution

- Tasks assigned based on role and expertise
- Weekly task review and redistribution if needed
- All team members contribute equally to documentation
