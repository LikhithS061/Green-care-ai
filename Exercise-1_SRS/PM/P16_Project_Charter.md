## Project Charter

### Project Title

Greencare AI: Personalized Plant Recommendation and Gardening Assistant

### Project Purpose

To develop an AI-powered application that helps home gardeners make informed
decisions about plant selection and disease management.

### Project Objectives

1. Provide personalized plant recommendations based on soil and climate data
2. Enable real-time disease diagnosis through image recognition
3. Reduce plant mortality rates for novice gardeners
4. Simplify the gardening learning curve
