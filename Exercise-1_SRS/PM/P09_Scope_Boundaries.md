## Scope Boundaries

### System Boundaries

1. The system operates on **user-provided data only**; it does not automatically
   sense soil moisture or environmental conditions through IoT devices.

2. The system **requires an active internet connection** for AI inference as
   models run on cloud servers.

3. Image uploads are limited to **5MB maximum** and must be in JPG/PNG format.

4. The system targets **home gardeners and hobbyists**, not commercial-scale
   agricultural operations.

### Data Boundaries

1. Plant database is limited to common garden plants suitable for home cultivation.
2. Disease detection is trained on common plant diseases visible on leaves/stems.
3. Location-based recommendations are limited to regions with available climate data.
